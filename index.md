<header class="hero">
  <h1>🛡️ Connect in Crisis</h1>
  <p>A multilingual guide for secure communication during shutdowns, surveillance, or blackouts — for people inside or outside the zone.</p>
</header>

<!-- English -->
<section dir="ltr" class="lang">
  <h2>English</h2>
  <h3>🧭 Purpose</h3>
  <p>To help people stay connected during a crisis — both those inside high-risk zones and those trying to reach them from outside. This guide is short, calm, and easy to use. Each step is practical and helps you feel more in control, even under stress.</p>
  <h3>📊 App Summary</h3>
  <div class="table-responsive">
    <table>
      <thead><tr><th>App</th><th>Mesh?</th><th>Offline?</th><th>Encryption</th><th>Best Use Case</th></tr></thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr><td>Bridgefy</td><td>✅ Bluetooth</td><td>✅</td><td>Moderate</td><td>Quick local messaging; not for sensitive info</td></tr>
        <tr><td>Briar</td><td>✅ Bluetooth/Wi‑Fi</td><td>✅</td><td>Strong</td><td>Trusted local groups (Android only)</td></tr>
        <tr><td>Session</td><td>❌</td><td>❌</td><td>Very strong</td><td>Anonymous long-distance communication</td></tr>
        <tr><td>SimpleX</td><td>❌</td><td>❌</td><td>Strong</td><td>Decentralized private messaging</td></tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
  </div>
  <h3>🛠️ Tier 1: Preparation (Before Shutdown)</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Install apps like Bridgefy, Briar, Session, or SimpleX.</li>
    <li>Write down phone numbers and shared emoji codes (🟢 = safe, 🔴 = need help) on paper.</li>
    <li>Make two simple plans: one for talking with people <em>inside</em> your area, one for <em>outside</em> contacts.</li>
    <li>Choose check-in times (e.g., 9 AM & 9 PM).</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>📡 Tier 2: During Blackout</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Use Bluetooth mesh (Bridgefy, Briar) for nearby messaging.</li>
    <li>Relay updates via anyone with internet access.</li>
    <li>Use tools like Gotenna or Meshtastic if available.</li>
    <li>Short clear messages: e.g., “Ali safe. Need water.”</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>🧠 Privacy Tips</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Keep your phone on you at all times.</li>
    <li>Use apps without real name or SIM requirements.</li>
    <li>Hide sensitive apps in folders or use disguise modes (e.g., Briar).</li>
    <li>Keep messages short with shared emojis or phrases.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
